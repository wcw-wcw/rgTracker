RGTracker

Privacy

RGTracker uses Riot Sign On only to verify that a player owns the Riot account they choose to link. VALORANT stats are shown only after that player opts in by connecting their Riot account.

Linked account data may include Riot ID, PUUID, and gameplay statistics returned by Riot APIs. RGTracker does not ask for or store Riot passwords.

Players can stop using RGTracker by signing out. Future production storage will include account unlinking and data deletion controls before public VALORANT profile hosting is enabled.
